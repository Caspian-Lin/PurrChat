# Product

## Register

product

## Users

PurrChat serves technical enthusiasts and AI explorers who want one calm space for real-time conversations with people and AI. In Bot Studio, creators configure, connect, validate, publish, and debug event-driven AI characters without leaving the chat product.

## Product Purpose

PurrChat combines a full-stack realtime messenger with a visual Bot Studio. Success means human and AI conversations feel equally native, while complex Bot behavior remains understandable, testable, and safe to publish.

## Brand Personality

Intimate, refined, and alive. The product should feel quiet and emotionally present rather than mechanical, while retaining the precision and trust expected from a technical creation tool.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, decorative card grids, neon AI tooling, purple-blue gradients, glassmorphism, gradient text, ornamental side stripes, and elastic motion. Do not expose controls that the runtime cannot execute or hide destructive state changes behind silent fallbacks.

## Design Principles

- Quiet confidence: establish hierarchy through clarity and restraint.
- Living geometry: use one coherent interaction vocabulary that feels approachable without becoming childish.
- Substance over surface: every control must support a real user task or system state.
- Breathing space: preserve focus in dense chat and workflow-authoring surfaces.
- Honest capability: the editor, validation, publishing, and runtime must agree on what is supported.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, meaningful status text that does not rely on color alone, responsive layouts from mobile through desktop, and `prefers-reduced-motion` for all non-essential transitions.
